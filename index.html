Our Story

Built on 30 Years of Grain Storage Expertise

From Deneysville to 7 African nations — Agrisilo has been engineering reliable grain infrastructure since the 1990s.

Africa's Grain Storage Partner

Agrisilo is a well-established silo company specialising in the import, installation and maintenance of grain storage and conveying systems. Underpinned by 30 years of hands-on industry experience, we combine deep technical knowledge with a practical understanding of agricultural infrastructure across Southern Africa.

Today, Agrisilo is a young, innovative, and continuously growing company — recognised as an expert and leader within the grain storage sector. Through consistent performance and a commitment to sustainable growth, we continue to strengthen our position across the continent.

Headquartered in Deneysville, Free State, South Africa, our teams operate across borders to deliver projects on time and to specification.
